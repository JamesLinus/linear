Welcome to Linear

We're thrilled to have you with us. Linear will help you achieve pixel-perfect designs without getting in your way.

In Linear, you work with rulers. Here is one. Go ahead, grab it around :-)

To manage your rulers, have a look at the lr icon in the tray bar, located on the top right corner of your screen. From there, you can create new rulers. You can create as many rulers as you want.

Shortcuts are really useful! To create a new ruler, press ^⌘R. To hide/show existing rulers, ^⌘T (toggle). To close a ruler, ⌘W.

Right-clicking on a ruler lets you duplicate it. It keeps the ruler's position and size.

In the settings window (available from clicking the lr icon in the tray bar,) you can change the displayed unit to either px or em.
